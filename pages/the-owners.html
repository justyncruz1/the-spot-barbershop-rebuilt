Our Story

Meet the Owners

Two brothers. One mission. Give every man who walks through those doors the best cut of his life.

Founders & Master Barbers

Manuel & Samuel
De La Torre

Manuel and Samuel De La Torre opened The Spot Barbershop with one thing in mind: the client always comes first. With that as the foundation, they built something most shops never do — a clientele that feels more like family than customers.

Growing up, the twins were — and still are — huge fans of boxing. Step through the doors and it's hard to miss. Portraits of Muhammad Ali and Mike Tyson line the walls. Movie posters and framed actors are everywhere — done intentionally so you get to know exactly who Manny and Sammy are before you even sit in the chair.

They pride themselves on being masters of their craft and being able to cater to both traditional and modern methods of barbering. Whether you want an old-fashioned hot towel shave or a modern skin fade, they will always do their very best to meticulously accommodate your needs.
