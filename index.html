Built by Barbers.
Run Like Family.

Manuel and Samuel De La Torre — twin brothers, master barbers, and boxing fans — opened The Spot with one rule: the client always comes first.

Step through the doors and you'll see portraits of Muhammad Ali and Mike Tyson on the walls, movie posters that tell you exactly who Manny and Sammy are, and a clientele that feels more like family than customers.

Whether you want an old-fashioned hot towel shave or a modern skin fade, they'll do their very best to meticulously accommodate your needs.
